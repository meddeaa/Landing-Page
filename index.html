1950s

The birth of rock 'n' roll with pioneers like Elvis Presley, Chuck Berry, and Little Richard. This era blended rhythm and blues with country music, creating a new sound that defined a generation.

Scroll to explore the decades

1960s

The British Invasion (Beatles, Rolling Stones), psychedelic rock, and the rise of guitar gods like Jimi Hendrix. Woodstock symbolized the counterculture movement tied to rock music.

1970s

The golden age of classic rock with Led Zeppelin, Pink Floyd, and The Who. Arena rock, progressive rock, and punk rock all emerged during this diverse decade.

1980s

Glam metal (Guns N' Roses, Mötley Crüe) and hard rock dominated, while alternative rock began to emerge. MTV revolutionized how people experienced music.

1990s

Grunge (Nirvana, Pearl Jam) brought raw emotion back to rock. Britpop (Oasis, Blur) and pop-punk (Green Day, Offspring) also defined the decade's rock landscape.
